Attached is a FERC Order issued today in Docket EL01-104-000.  The decision
concludes that, when ATC would permit, Transmission Providers must allow
firm redirection of long-term, point-to-point transmission service for
periods less than the remainder of the contract.  SPP's practice has been to
require that any redirection of transmission service on a firm basis should
be for the remaining balance of the term of service.  Effective immediately,
SPP is implementing the FERC Order for redirection of long-term,
point-to-point transmission service .  SPP's business practices will be
revised and posted on SPP's OASIS.  If you have any questions, please call
myself or Lanny Nickell.
